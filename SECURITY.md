# Security Policy

## Responsible Disclosure

If you believe information in this repository concerns your organization or if you would like to discuss a potential exposure related to our research, please contact:

security@sentrytrace.com

Please include:

- organization name
- affected asset
- description of the exposure

We will review the information and coordinate disclosure responsibly.

---

## Scope

This repository documents exposure research using passive reconnaissance techniques.

Examples published here are:

- redacted
- sanitized
- shared for research purposes

No sensitive operational information is intentionally disclosed.
