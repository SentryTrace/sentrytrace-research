# Attack Surface Discovery Methodology

This research focuses on identifying exposed digital assets through passive reconnaissance.

The goal is to map the external attack surface visible from the public internet.

---

## Research techniques

Common passive reconnaissance techniques include:

- subdomain discovery
- DNS enumeration
- API indexing analysis
- search engine discovery
- OSINT infrastructure mapping

---

## Exposure categories

Common exposure types observed include:

- administrative dashboards
- internal documents
- development or staging systems
- management panels
- internal APIs

---

## Research approach

The methodology prioritizes:

- passive observation
- responsible documentation
- redaction of sensitive data

No intrusive testing or service disruption techniques are used.
