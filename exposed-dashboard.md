# Exposed Management Dashboard

## Summary

During passive reconnaissance an administrative dashboard was discovered accessible without authentication.

The interface appeared to provide operational visibility into internal platform activity.

---

## Discovery vector

The dashboard was identified through passive subdomain discovery and infrastructure indexing analysis.

---

## Risk

Publicly accessible management panels may expose sensitive operational capabilities such as:

- user management
- financial information
- operational metrics

If left unprotected these interfaces may represent a significant security risk.
