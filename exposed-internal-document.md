\# Exposed Internal Governance Document



\## Summary



During passive reconnaissance an internal governance document was discovered accessible through a public web endpoint.



The document contained internal organizational information and operational planning details.



\---



\## Discovery vector



The document was identified through enumeration of publicly accessible media objects exposed by a web application API.



The API response revealed a publicly accessible file stored in a web uploads directory.



\---



\## Risk



Publicly accessible internal documents may expose:



\- operational meeting information

\- executive roles

\- internal planning details



This information can facilitate targeted phishing campaigns or corporate intelligence gathering.



\---



\## Evidence



A redacted screenshot of the document is available in the screenshots directory.



All sensitive information has been removed.
